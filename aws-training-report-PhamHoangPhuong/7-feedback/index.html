Sharing and Feedback

Throughout my participation in the First Cloud AI Journey program, I had many valuable experiences. I would like to sincerely share my feedback and suggestions to help the FCAJ team continue improving the program for future interns.

Overall Evaluation

1. Working Environment
The working environment at FCAJ is friendly, open, and highly supportive. Team members were always willing to help whenever I encountered difficulties, even outside working hours. The workspace is modern, well-organized, and comfortable, providing an excellent environment for learning and productivity. However, I believe the program could be further improved by organizing more team-building activities, cultural events, or informal gatherings to strengthen relationships among team members, especially interns.

2. Mentor and Team Admin Support
I highly appreciate the support provided by both my mentor and the team administrators. My mentor was patient, explained concepts clearly, and always encouraged me to ask questions and solve problems independently instead of simply providing answers. This approach helped me develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills. The team administrators also provided excellent support with documentation, resources, and administrative matters, making it easy for interns to adapt and work effectively.

3. Relevance to My Major
The tasks assigned during the First Cloud AI Journey project were highly relevant to my field of study. They strengthened my knowledge of cloud computing while introducing me to modern cloud infrastructure, artificial intelligence, and practical industry tools. This allowed me to connect academic knowledge with real-world applications.

4. Learning and Skill Development Opportunities
This is one of the strongest aspects of the program. Throughout the internship, I developed many new skills, including project collaboration, technical documentation, software testing, professional communication, and presentation skills. My mentor also shared practical experience from real projects, helping me gain a clearer understanding of my future career path.

5. Company Culture and Teamwork
FCAJ has a positive and collaborative working culture. Team members respect one another, work professionally, and maintain a friendly atmosphere. Whenever urgent tasks arose, everyone worked together regardless of position or experience. As an intern, I always felt respected and treated as an important member of the team.

6. Internship Policies and Benefits
The internship program offers flexible working hours and provides interns with opportunities to participate in internal training sessions alongside full-time employees. These benefits greatly motivate interns and create a positive learning experience.

Additional Questions

  • What did you enjoy the most during your internship?
    The most rewarding part of my internship was working in a supportive environment with dedicated mentors while contributing to a meaningful project like First Cloud AI Journey. I could clearly see my improvement throughout the program.
  • What do you think should be improved for future interns?
    I suggest providing a more detailed onboarding program during the first weeks, together with introductory documentation and a structured training schedule. In addition, more experience-sharing sessions from senior engineers would be very beneficial.
  • Would you recommend this internship program to your friends? Why?
    Yes. I would definitely recommend this internship because it provides a professional working environment, supportive mentors, and valuable opportunities to gain practical experience in Cloud Computing and Artificial Intelligence. It is an excellent place for students to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry practice.

Suggestions and Expectations

  • Suggestions for improving the internship experience:
    Provide a clearer learning roadmap for each intern from the beginning of the program, organize more hands-on workshops covering modern cloud and AI technologies, and schedule regular one-on-one feedback sessions between mentors and interns.
  • Would you like to continue participating in this program in the future?
    Yes. I would be delighted to continue participating in future phases of the First Cloud AI Journey program or related projects, allowing me to deepen my knowledge and contribute further to the team.
  • Additional comments:
    I hope the program can expand its internship opportunities and introduce more independent mini-projects for interns. It would also be valuable to organize a final sharing session where interns can exchange experiences and lessons learned with one another.
  • Overall, I would like to express my sincere gratitude to the entire FCAJ team, especially my mentor and the team administrators, for their continuous guidance and support throughout my internship. This has truly been a meaningful and unforgettable learning journey.
